care tips

Be careful before the procedure

Never, under any circumstances, trim your eyebrows and eyelashes with scissors!

Try not to use makeup before the procedure

Do not pluck your eyebrows yourself at least a week before visiting a specialist

Care after the procedure

Use special oils to care for eyelashes and eyebrows

It is not prohibited to use cosmetics!

Use a special brush to comb your eyebrows and eyelashes
